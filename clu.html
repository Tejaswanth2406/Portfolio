001 / ABOUT

WHAT
IS
CLU?

"Most people never read what they agree to.
CLU does."

CLU is an AI-powered web application that analyzes legal documents in seconds. It surfaces danger clauses, forced arbitration traps, data-selling permissions, auto-renewal schemes, and IP rights grabs — the things buried in paragraph 47 that companies count on you never reading.

Built with React 18, TypeScript, and powered by either Claude (Anthropic) or GPT-4o mini (OpenAI) — your choice. Your API key. Your data stays on your device. No tracking. No logs. No servers.

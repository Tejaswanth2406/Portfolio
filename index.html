About

Who
I Am

I'm a 3rd year Computer Science student at GITAM University, Visakhapatnam — Core CSE, working at the intersection of offensive security and applied AI. I don't just study how systems behave. I study how they break, why they break, and what it takes to make them impossible to break the same way twice.

My interests span cybersecurity, AI & LLMs, RAG systems, security research, and cloud security. My current flagship work is Hollow Purple — a research-grade adaptive cloud identity detection platform that models GCP IAM as live temporal graphs and surfaces multi-stage attack chains with sub-second latency.

Methodology

Digital
Bio-mimicry

Distributed
Intelligence

Much like the nine brains of an octopus, my approach to AI focuses on distributed agentic orchestration. I build systems where specialized models operate with local autonomy yet contribute to a unified cognitive goal, ensuring resilience and complex problem-solving capabilities.

Offensive
Velocity

In security research, speed is a defensive necessity. The marlin represents my philosophy of high-velocity offensive research — identifying and neutralizing threats before they can settle. I prioritize precision-guided exploitation to surface critical vulnerabilities at scale.

Collective
Defense

True security isn't found in a single wall, but in collective intelligence. By leveraging RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) and swarm-based detection, I create defensive ecosystems that learn from every interaction, mimicking the coordinated movement of a school of fish.

Verifiable
Integrity

Security evidence should survive pressure. I design integrity around hash-chained events, replayable state, and clear audit trails, so every finding can be traced from signal to source instead of depending on trust alone.

Cloud Security
Operations

Cloud infrastructure requires a highly adaptive, multi-layered defensive posture. I focus on implementing robust IAM governance, continuous monitoring, and automated threat response across complex, distributed environments to keep operations resilient and auditable.
